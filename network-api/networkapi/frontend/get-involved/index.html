Fellowships

Our Fellowships empower bright minds in the fields of public policy, science and journalism. We partner with the world’s leading NGOs. Among them: Citizen Lab, Privacy International, EDRi, Freedom of the Press Foundation, Data & Society and Derechos Digitales.

Local Organizing

We connect passionate people in cities across five continents, from technologists in Chattanooga to educators in Cape Town. Our Network provides organizing best-practices and mutual support. In certain cities, our Network can also deploy on-the-ground staff.
