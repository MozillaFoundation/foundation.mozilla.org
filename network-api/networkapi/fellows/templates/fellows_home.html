The Fellowship is a global change-making and leadership development opportunityies for activists, creators, and leaders at the core of the internet health movement. The Fellowship is collaborative, multi-disciplinary program that provides the opportunity for leaders from across various backgrounds to pursue projects and increase their influence on the field to create long-term impact.

Why Become a Fellow?

The Mozilla Fellows have a unique opportunity to collaborate with a global community of internet health leaders to pursue passion projects and increase their influence in advancing internet health. Key features of the program include:

  • Opportunities to speak and publish to increase awareness about important internet health issues.
  • Building impactful working relationships with an interdisciplinary network of leaders of the open web.
  • Designing an impactful project to support the field of internet health.
  • Learning from and collaborating with a global community of Mozillians.

Expectations

While there are some nuances related to particular Fellowship focus areas, all Fellows are expected to be independent, community-motivated collaborators that are committed to improve the internet for all. Some of the shared expectations include:

  • Attendance at a key set of events throughout the year.
  • Accountability to executing a project with a tangible product focused on improving internet health.
  • A commitment to work on the fellowship full-time for at least 10 months.
  • Serving as an advocate for internet health in the community.
